Weather Normalized Saving Calculation Methodology

The weather normalized saving for each action or group of action is computed with the three inputs

  • Monthly energy: from EUAS data set
  • Monthly average temperature: from PI system
  • ECM action
    • excel: Covered_Facilities_All Energy mmBTUs_FY14_EISA07Sec432
    • CTS
    • excel: Light-Touch M_V
    • excel: Portfolio HPGB Dashboard
    • excel: GSAlink_Buildings_First_55

The computing process is

  1. tidy data: tidy the ECM action inputs into such a table format and group actions by building_number and substantial_completion_date
  2. define evaluation period: the period before "substantial_completion_date" of an action or a group of actions up to 3 years is defined as the "pre" period, the time period after the "substantial_completion_date" of an action or a group of actions is defined as the "post" period
  3. compute lean regression for the "pre" period and return: regression parameters, error estimates, actual measurement, etc.
  4. compute estimated consumption: for the "post" period, compute the estimated monthly consumption by applying the "pre" period model on the monthly average temperature of the "post" period.
  5. Aggregate the monthly of 2 to 3 year period to one year for both the "pre" and "post" period: Compute the average actual monthly consumption (group by month, aggregate by mean), A_i (i in {1, 2, ..., 12}). Compute the average estimated monthly consumption, E_i (i = {1, 2, ..., 12})
  6. compute saving:
    total actual consumption A = \sum(A_i)
    total estimated consumption E = \sum(E_i)
    saving percent = (E - A)/E
    saving amount = E - A
