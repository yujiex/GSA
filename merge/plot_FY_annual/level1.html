Total Energy Trend

EUI trend of A + I buildings in EUAS data set with robust energy data, only consider Gas + Electric for EUI (24% reduction till 2015, 18% reduction till 2013)

Assumptions

  • The buildings plotted are the set of A + I buildingss that have at least 6 years of robust energy data (Electric EUI >= 12 kBtu/sq.ft and Gas EUI >= 3 kBtu/sq.ft) from FY2007 to FY2015.
  • The EUI is calculated as total Electric + Gas consumption divided by total square footage
  • The category uses the record in the most recent year
  • The square footage uses the record of the corresponding year (not averaged across years)

EUI trend of A + C + I buildings in EUAS data set using raw Category designation, EUI computed including all energy types (23% reduction till 2015, 20% reduction till 2013)

Assumptions

  • The buildings plotted are A + C + I buildingss in EUAS data set.
  • The EUI is calculated as total Electric + Gas + Steam + Oil consumption divided by total square footage
  • The square footage uses the record of the corresponding year (not averaged across years)
  • The category uses the record of the corresponding year (not the most recent record)
